Tax

Non Resident Indians under FEMA :- An Indian Citizen who stays abroad for (a) employment/ carrying on business or (b) vacation outside India or (c) stays abroad under circumstances indicating an intention for an uncertain duration of stay abroad is a non-resident.  Persons posted in U.N. organizations and officials deputed abroad by Central/ State Government and Public Sector Undertakings on temporary assignments are also treated as non-resident. Non-resident foreign citizens of Indian Origin are treated on par with non-resident Indian citizens. NON RESIDENT INDIANS UNDER INCOME TAX ACT :   The laws in this regard are quite complicated as these it  does not  define who is non resident. Rather these define who is resident and who are not ordinarily resident.  Therefore, if a person does not fall in the category of resident or not ordinarily resident, he / she will be non-resident.
